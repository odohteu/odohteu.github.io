Visualization

These datasets contain data from Europen Union Commission about victims of human traffic in 2013 and 2014 as the result of the first anti-trafficking report by the institution (2016). The datasets contain information regarding the total number of victims, the forms of exploitation, the age combined to the gender (thus the share of men, boys, girls and women). The spatial coverage of the dataset are the European Union member states.

To represent these datasets we used pie charts to show composition: circles cut in segments to show parts and their proportion with respect to the whole, and to other parts.
