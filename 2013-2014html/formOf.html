Visualization

This dataset contain data from Europen Union Commission about victims of human traffic in 2013 and 2014 as the result of the first anti-trafficking report by the institution (2016). The spatial coverage of the dataset are the European Union member states.

In this pie chart are represented most frequent forms of exploitation of victims of human trafficking, in percentages on the total.
