Visualization

This dataset contains data from Europen Union Commission about victims of human traffic in 2017 and 2018 as the result of the Data collection on trafficking in human beings in the EU, report of 2020.

Specifically, is reported the majority status of victims, so the total counts of adult victims and child victims, for each country of the European Union. The spatial coverage of the dataset are the European Union member states.
