Visualization

This dataset contains data from the United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime (UNODC) about victims of human traffic from 2003 to 2017. The dataset contains information regarding the total number of victims per member State of the European Union and European Region. The spatial coverage of the dataset are the European Union member states.

We chose to represent the total counts of victims in an animated horizontal bar chart, as it is easier to show comparisons among countries and along the years.
