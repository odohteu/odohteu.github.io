Visualization

These datasets contain data from Europen Union Commission about victims of human traffic in 2015 and 2016 as the result of the Data collection on trafficking in human beings in the EU, report of 2018. The datasets contain information regarding the total number of victims, their majority status (with a specification on children), gender, destination country and citizenship of registered victims (both EU and non-EU); in the European Union member states.

We decided which visualization was best suited to represent a dataset based on what we wanted to show. For instance, when we wanted to show comparisons we used bar charts and line charts. To show composition, we used pie charts to represent simple share of total.
