This dataset contains data from Europen Union Commission about victims of human traffic in 2015 and 2016 as the result of the Data collection on trafficking in human beings in the EU, report of 2018.

Specifically, is reported the majority status of victims, for each of the EU member States. The spatial coverage of the dataset are the European Union member states.
